Spectrum Installation is a mid-size company located in Bradenton, Florida. We install over 1,000 Residential and Commercial projects each year. All projects, large or small, are completed by background checked crews who specialize in the type of flooring being installed.

Since Spectrums inception, our mission has been to meet the following objectives on each installation we undertake:

  • Guarantee (SEE BELOW) your installation will be done correctly and in a timely fashion.
  • Continuous coordination between our office and the customer, along with great care from our installers, allows Spectrum to
  • minimize the inconveniences / disruptions that may typically be associated with a flooring upgrade.
  • The project (large or small) will be completed by only the best technical installers who hold exceptional reviews from past and repeat customers.

Spectrum Installation, started by Mark and Lavonne Dimitroff, opened its’ Florida operation in 2005. We came to Florida to fulfil a Lowes Home Improvement agreement to install flooring and wall tile for Lowe’s customers

Since that time, we are proud to have watched Spectrum grow to a mid-size company. As an installation company we understand that to a large extent we are only as good as the partners we deploy to complete an installation. In that regard, our crews install exclusively for our company, a significant number of who have been with us since the company’s beginning.
