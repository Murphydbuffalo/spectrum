Spectrum Installation, started by Mark and Lavonne Dimitroff, opened its’ Florida operation in 2005. We came to Florida to fulfil a Lowes Home Improvement agreement to install flooring and wall tile for Lowe’s customers

Since that time, we are proud to have watched Spectrum grow to a mid-size company. As an installation company we understand that to a large extent we are only as good as the partners we deploy to complete an installation. In that regard, our crews install exclusively for our company, a significant number of who have been with us since the company’s beginning.
